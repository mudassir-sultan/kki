Our Story

Karakoram International is your one stop solution for textile sourcing and spare parts trading.We have years of experience in the field of Textile Sourcing. We want to be one of the leading Trading and Buying house in Pakistan of Textile Products. Karakoram International serving as a trading and buying house serving markets in Japan, USA, Europe, the Sub Continent and the Middle East. Our main focus is Complete Customer Satisfaction and we have clients all over the world that are taking advantage of our experience and expertise in the field of Textile Sourcing. We also import spare parts for European Truck Parts and specialized in Volvo.

Karakoram International was established by Mr. Wasim Ali Khan in 2001, as a trading and buying house serving markets in Japan, USA, Europe, the Sub Continent and the Middle East. Presently it offers a unique combination of essential services to market, promote, and facilitate the exports of Pakistani home textiles and garments to their Principals, which include sourcing suppliers, arranging meetings, communicating between Buyer/supplier, negotiating orders, and controlling Buyer’s specification, quality and delivery dates.

Karakoram International is a one window for textile and apparel sourcing and we are also one of the leading importers of Spare Parts of Volvo vehicles and machinery.
